"What's Pooh spelled backwards?"

Derrick Martell Rose, nicknamed "Pooh", is a point guard for the New York Knicks. A Chi-town native, Rose played his first 7 seasons in the NBA in the Windy City with the Chicago Bulls. Before that, Rose was a point guard at The University of Memphis where he led the Tigers to the 2008 NCAA Finals.
