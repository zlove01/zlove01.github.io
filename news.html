Derrick Rose was traded to the Knicks for a second time. The trade sent Dennis Smith Jr. and a 2021 second round pick (via Charlotte) to the Pistons. Rose will reunite with his old coach Tom Thibodeau who won Coach of the Year in 2011. Read the full trade details here.

10/31/18 Derrick Rose Drops 50!

It was a very scary Halloween for the Utah Jazz defense as Derrick Rose set a new career high in scoring with 50 points. Rose shot 19/31 from the field, 4/7 from deep, and 8/11 from the free throw line. Outside of scoring rose had 4 rebounds, 6 assists, 1 steal, and a game winning block. Stats via Basketball Reference.
